# Design System Document

## Theme Overview
Our design system is built on a foundation of clarity and user-centricity. We aim for a consistent and intuitive experience across all platforms, balancing aesthetic appeal with functional efficiency. This document outlines the core principles and specifications that guide our visual language.

## Color Palette
The primary color palette is designed to evoke trust and dynamism, with a focus on accessibility and brand recognition.

-   **Primary Color:** `#007AFF` - A vibrant blue, used for key interactive elements, calls to action, and brand accents.
-   **Secondary Color:** `#E53935` - A bold red, employed for secondary actions, alerts, and complementary visual elements.
-   **Tertiary Color:** `#607D8B` - A muted blue-grey, serving as an additional accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements.
-   **Neutral Color:** `#F8F9FA` - A very light grey, providing a clean and subtle background for content and surfaces.

## Typography
Our typography system is chosen for readability and character, ensuring information is presented clearly and engagingly.

-   **Headlines:** `Manrope` - Chosen for its modern and geometric aesthetic, providing a strong visual hierarchy for titles and headings.
-   **Body Text:** `Inter` - A highly legible and versatile typeface, ideal for long-form content and general UI text.
-   **Labels:** `Inter` - Consistent with body text for clarity and cohesion in interactive elements and form fields.

## Spacing and Layout
We utilize a considered spacing system to ensure visual balance and create a comfortable reading experience.

-   **Spacing:** `Normal` (2) - A balanced approach to whitespace, providing adequate separation between elements without feeling overly sparse or dense. This level promotes good readability and visual organization.

## Roundedness
The corner radius of our UI elements contributes to the overall aesthetic feel of our interface.

-   **Roundedness:** `Moderate` (2) - A balanced level of roundedness applied to UI components, offering a friendly and approachable feel while maintaining a professional appearance.
